Top three activities to do at Lagos

lekki conservation center

The lekki conservation park in Lekki, is an oasis of nature within the urban chaos of Lagos, and the suspended canopy walkway that rises over the reserve is the longest in all of Africa.

lekki Art and Craft Market

Lekki Arts & Craft market consists of well organised mini stores that sell pieces from carvings to metal work to corals to painting to pearls and much more.
